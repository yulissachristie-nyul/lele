a note for you.

tuesday · 19 may 2026

I know you wanna cry scrolling all these, but no, not yet.
Here comes the best part.

It's a day not to be celebrated the big way,
but just this little and small and often forgotten way (my way).

I always the one who appreciate the small things, a firm believer that life's full of the small things, not the big things.

So what are the smalls for you?

While in my opinion, to you —

small means

you showing up everyday, even when God knows how tiring and painful and not easy it is.

small means

every "akh we're not there yet" and every "try again".

small means

every bouncy step you take on your run and your everyday life in work, and everything.
sometimes the road is smooth aspal, but sometimes you have to cross the road, and sometimes you have to step on rocks and muds.

small means

every small post, every small or casual words from others that slowly coming to you and help you cross off your longtime dreams one by one.

small means

you finally taking things as it is — because life's meant to celebrate the small things, not only and always the big big things.

life's not gonna get easier.
and what's ahead — well, no one really knows.
but you got this another small creature, in front of you (more often beside you), that will help you cross off more small things in this life.

happy (not so little) birthday.

okay, you can cry now.
